## About

I enjoy the full arc of a problem — from a model that perceives the world, to the service that puts it to work, to the infrastructure that keeps it running. Most of my background sits at the intersection of computer vision, robotics, and machine learning, but I'm just as at home building backends, shipping frontends, and wiring up the systems in between.

This profile collects my public work. I'm steadily organizing and documenting each repository, so it doubles as a tour of what I've been building and thinking about.
